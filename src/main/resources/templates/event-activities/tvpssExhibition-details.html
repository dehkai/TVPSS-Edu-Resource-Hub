More Information About the Videographer Role

As a videographer, you will be responsible for:

  • Capturing school events and student activities.
  • Editing and producing high-quality videos for various segments.
  • Collaborating with the program team to ensure creative vision is achieved.
  • Maintaining equipment and ensuring proper storage of video files.

Skills required include:

  • Proficiency in video editing software (e.g., Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro).
  • Strong creative and storytelling skills.
  • Attention to detail and ability to meet deadlines.
